Green

Green is a color located in the rainbow somewhere between blue and yellow and is the color of St. Patrick's day and green m&ms.
Green is a color mostly ignored or overlooked because of its ability to become some really ugly shades. However, it is still more pleasant to the eyes than yellow and many shades of orange.

Why green?

Green is the color of plants.
Plants give us life.
Therefore green gives us life.

Green has been recognized in pop culture to symbolize

  • radioactivity
  • the military
  • sickness
  • nature
  • envy
  • greed

and most importantly:

  • shrek
