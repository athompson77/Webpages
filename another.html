Freshness

Freshness is the sweet smell of nature and clean linen that is created by the manmade element Febreze.

Febreze

Febreze is the miracle of engineering that creates freshness in your homes, cars, and sock drawers.
For some subjects, it creates the illusion and feeling of being outside when in reality he or she has not left the house or car in days.

How is this possible?

Scientists in the fast growing field of scent production have manufactured a chemical that, when sprayed onto the origin of odor, becomes S.W.A.G.G., which stands for Switftly Wafting Airborne Germicidal Gasiform. This main ingredient in Febreze creates the magical freshness that engulfs the previous stank.

However, due to the wide range of possible applications of Febreze and its easy accessibility, some may find themselves confused as to the situations in which Febreze's magical powers can used in a socially acceptable manner.

Luckily, this dilemma has been anticipated by the the committee for safe scent usage and they have created a table specifically for those stuck in such situations.
